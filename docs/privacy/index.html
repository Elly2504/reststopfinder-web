Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

RestStop Finder ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Location Data

The App uses your device's location to find nearby highway rest stops. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers. When you grant "Always" location permission, the App uses iOS region monitoring to notify you of nearby rest stops while driving — this also runs on-device.

You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

The App queries the OpenStreetMap Overpass API to retrieve rest stop data. These queries include geographic coordinates based on your approximate area to find nearby rest stops. No personal identifiers, device IDs, or account information are included in these requests. OpenStreetMap's own privacy policy applies to their service.

No other third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Favorites — saved via SwiftData, stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences — notification settings and onboarding state, stored in UserDefaults.
  • Cache — rest stop data is cached temporarily to reduce network requests. You can clear the cache from the Settings tab.

No data is synced to external servers or cloud services.

No Accounts or Personal Information

The App does not require an account, does not collect your name or email, and does not store any personal information.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children. No personal information is collected from any user.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data (favorites, preferences, cache) is stored locally on your device. You can delete your favorites individually from the Favorites tab, clear the cache from Settings, or delete all app data by uninstalling the App. No data is retained on any server.

Your Rights

Since RestStop Finder does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete remotely. All data is under your control on your device. You can revoke location access, delete favorites, or uninstall the App at any time.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
